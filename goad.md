Referrals: the unfair advantage
At Tier 1, cold application response rate is ~2%. With a referral, it's ~15–20%. 10x difference. This is where you spend time that others don't.
Your referral playbook (Week 1)
Export your LinkedIn connections. Filter by current company among the 20 above.
For each match, send this DM (personalize first line):
"Hey [Name], saw your post on [X]. Congrats on the [company] role. I'm a SWE at IndiaMART (2 yrs, ex-Logy.ai, IIIT Jabalpur), looking to move — built ArogyaCare (agentic AI med platform) and led the call pipeline handling 150K recordings/day at IndiaMART. Would you be open to a referral for [specific role link]? Happy to share resume."
No connections at a target? Search "[Company] software engineer" on LinkedIn, filter by 2nd-degree, connect with a short note. After they accept → DM asking for referral. Response rate is ~20%. Send 15, get 3 referrals. Do this in Week 1.


Have a referral champion from IIIT Jabalpur? Post in your batch/college alumni WhatsApp groups asking for referrals at target companies. This is the #1 source for many IIIT grads.


Target: 10 referrals obtained by end of Week 2. This matters more than any DSA problem you'll solve in that window.

Weeks 7–9 — Peak interview mode
At this point you should have 3–6 active interview loops. Your time shifts heavily to interview prep + mocks.
DSA: Maintenance — 1–2 problems/day + company-specific prep (LeetCode company tags for each company you're interviewing with)
System design: 2 full designs/week on paper with 45-min timer
Mocks: 2/week (mix of Pramp free + interviewing.io paid)
Post-interview habit: IMMEDIATELY after each interview, write down every question asked. This is the highest-ROI study material you'll ever have.
Realistic conversion: If you get into 8 interview loops, expect 2–4 to result in offers. That's normal. Don't panic at rejections — they're data.
Weeks 10–11 — Close + negotiate
Never accept first offer. Ask for 72 hours, always.
Try to stack 2 offers in the same week. Competing offers = ₹5–10L+ upside.
Negotiation reading: Levels.fyi threads + Haseeb Qureshi's "Ten Rules for Negotiating a Job Offer"
Key negotiation anchors: (a) competing offer number, (b) your current CTC + 40% delta, (c) specific desire ("I'm targeting 48 LPA total comp")
Week 12 — Serve notice, transition
Sign offer, then resign from IndiaMART (never resign first)
Negotiate notice period buyout if needed (sometimes new company will pay)
Use 30-day notice to smoothly hand off; leave on good terms (marketplace is small)
